## üìç Best Ad Placements for StreamVault

### High-Performing Ad Positions:

1. **Above the Fold (Top of page)**
   - Banner ad (728x90 or 970x90)
   - Before show listings
   - **Highest earnings**

2. **In-Content Ads**
   - Between show cards
   - After every 3-4 shows
   - Native ads that blend in

3. **Sidebar Ads**
   - Vertical banner (300x600)
   - Sticky sidebar ad
   - Good for desktop

4. **Below Video Player**
   - Banner ad (728x90)
   - After episode description
   - High visibility

5. **End of Page**
   - Before footer
   - Lower earnings but still valuable

### Avoid:
- ‚ùå Too many ads (reduces user experience)
- ‚ùå Ads that block content
- ‚ùå Pop-ups (against AdSense policy)
- ‚ùå More than 3 ads per page
